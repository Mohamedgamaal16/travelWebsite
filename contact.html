Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking international trips 3-6 months in advance for the best prices and availability. For domestic travel, 1-2 months is usually sufficient.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, as well as PayPal and bank transfers.

Can I make changes to my booking?

Yes, most bookings can be modified. Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss changes. Some restrictions may apply depending on the airline or accommodation provider.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options that cover trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost luggage. We highly recommend purchasing insurance for all international travel.
